Simple Lasagna Recipe

Description

A classic and comforting lasagna made with layers of tender lasagna noodles, a savory ground beef and marinara sauce, creamy ricotta cheese mixed with egg, and gooey mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. This easy-to-make dish is perfect for a hearty family meal and can be baked to a golden perfection with a crispy top layer.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 (15-ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  4. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat. Stir in the marinara sauce and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese with the egg. Mix well.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Place 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread one-third of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by one-third of the mozzarella cheese. Spoon a layer of meat sauce over the cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers two more times, ending with a layer of meat sauce. Sprinkle the top with grated Parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
