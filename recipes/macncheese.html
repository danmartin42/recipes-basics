Simple Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

A creamy and delicious mac and cheese recipe combining tender elbow macaroni with a smooth cheese sauce made from sharp cheddar and Parmesan cheeses. This easy recipe can be topped with breadcrumbs and baked for a crispy finish or enjoyed as is for a classic, creamy comfort food. Ideal for a quick weeknight dinner or a family gathering.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs (optional, for topping)
